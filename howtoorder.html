How To Order

We deliver your customized clothing at your convenient place and time.

The process

Step 1: Decide the type of clothing you want stitched after looking at the swatches

Step 2: Call us or email us with your convenient time and place

Step 3: Our representative would come to you and take your measurements after ensuring your preference in fabric, style, design and clothing

Step 4: The first fitting session is complementary and our representative would come to ensure you get the desired fitting

Step 5: Delivery would be made at your convenient place and time
