FAQs

Q. How to place the order?
A. Please contact us either through email or phone to book an appointment. Please let us know the type of clothing you want stitched and we would approach you at your convenient location with the relevant material swatches.Â 

Q. How do I know if the stitched clothing is as per my measurement?

A. You will be given a copy of the measurement sheet at the time of measurement. The same sheet will be given for stitching and will be available with us to ensure the right fit.


Q. Can I give my own fabric for stitching?

A. Yes, you can give your fabric for stitching.


Q. Can I give my well-fitting garment for measurement?

A. Yes, you can give your well-fitting garment for measurement. The garment will be stitched as per the fitting of the garment given by you.


Q. How and when to make the payment?

A. Part payment for material bought has to be made at the time of measurement session. Currently we take only cash payment. Complete payment is to be made at the time of delivery.


Q. How can I cancel the order?

A. You can cancel the order anytime before the measurement taking session because after that the product would be sent for stitching as per your size. Once the stitching process has started we would not be able to cancel the order.
