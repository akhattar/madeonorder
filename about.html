About us

A group of fashion enthusiasts who like clothes that fit well, with great styling and are value for money. We would like to share the same passion with you. We provide customized tailoring that meets your expectations and sense of style.

About the founders:

We are a group of friends who studied from IIM Kozhikode with work experience in different sectors like FMCG, Retail, Education and E-commerce. Our goal is to show you the difference a well-tailored garment can make to your personality. We believe that once you have found your perfect fit you would not want to go back to the readymade garment.

We want you to experience classic styling and awesome fitting at the convenience of your home.
