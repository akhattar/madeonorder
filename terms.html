Terms and Conditions

Quality assurance

In a rare chance of you not being satisfied with the fitting, we will make sure that we match the measurement that was approved by you at the time of measurement taking session. Moreover, we promise that the material that you approve at the time of product selection is the material that we use for your finished garment.


Privacy policy

Your personal details like your name, telephone number, email address, etc will not be shared with a third party. These details are only for our database which would help us serve you better in the future.


Delivery

All products are customized and made only for you. Delivery time may vary from order to order and your availability for measurement taking and fitting sessions. An indicative time period is approximately 10 days.


Return policy

We ensure that you receive the perfect fitting every time you buy our garment. As the garments are tailor-made for you, based on your preferences, we do not accept returns.
